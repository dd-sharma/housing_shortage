# Data Sources

## Housing Supply Data

1. **Number of Permits Issued**
    * Total building permits (residential, single-family, multi-family)
        * Data Source: [Department of Housing and Urban Development: Residential Construction Permits by County](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/residential-construction-permits-by-county)
  
2. **Type of Construction**
     * Single-family homes
         * Data Source: [Department of Housing and Urban Development: Residential Construction Permits by County](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/residential-construction-permits-by-county)
     * Multi-family units (duplexes, triplexes, apartments)
         * Data Source: [Department of Housing and Urban Development: Residential Construction Permits by County](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/residential-construction-permits-by-county)

3. **Unit Counts**
     * Number of housing units associated with each permit
         * Data Source: [Department of Housing and Urban Development: Residential Construction Permits by County](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/residential-construction-permits-by-county)

## Demand Data

1. **Population Growth and Demographics**
     * Historical population
         * Data Source: [Federal Reserve Bank Economic Data: Residential Population in MSAs](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/searchresults/?st=Resident%20Population%20in)
     * Projected population
         * Data Source: [Census Bureau: Metropolitan and Micropolitan Statistical Areas Population Totals: 2020-2023](https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/popest/2020s-total-metro-and-micro-statistical-areas.html)
  
2. **Economic Indicators**
     * Wages
         * Data Source: [Federal Reserve Bank Economic Data: Average Weekly Wages for Employees in Total Covered Establishments in MSA](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/searchresults/?st=Average%20Weekly%20Wages%20for%20Employees%20in%20Total%20Covered%20Establishments%20in)
     * Unemployment rates
         * Data Source: [Federal Reserve Bank Economic Data: Unemployment Rate in MSA](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/searchresults/?st=Unemployment%20Rate%20in)

3. **Migration Trends**
     * Migration In & Out of MSAs
         * Data Source: [Census Bureau: American Community Survey - Geographical Mobility in the Past Year for Current Residence, Metropolitan Statistical Area Level](https://data.census.gov/table/ACSDT1Y2023.B07201?q=American%20Community%20Survey&g=010XX00US$3100000)
